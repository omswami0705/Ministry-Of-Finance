Key Components of the Union Budget

1. Revenue Budget

Includes tax and non-tax revenues such as income tax, GST, excise duties, and dividends from public sector enterprises.

2. Capital Budget

Focuses on government borrowings, disinvestment proceeds, and investments in infrastructure and defense.

3. Expenditure Budget

Covers government spending on salaries, subsidies, social welfare schemes, and public sector investments.

4. Fiscal Deficit & Debt Management

Details the borrowing plan, deficit control measures, and debt repayment strategy.

5. Sectoral Allocations

Funds allocated to sectors like education, healthcare, infrastructure, defense, and agriculture.

6. Policy Announcements

New tax policies, subsidy reforms, economic stimulus plans, and privatization strategies.
