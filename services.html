Citizen Services

Taxation Services

  • Income Tax Portal - File your income tax returns online.
  • GST Portal - Manage GST registration and filings.
  • Customs & Excise - Access import/export duty calculators.

Banking & Financial Inclusion

  • Jan Dhan Yojana - Open zero-balance bank accounts.
  • Digital Payments - Support for UPI, BHIM, and online payments.
  • MSME & Mudra Loans - Apply for business and startup loans.

Investment & Savings

  • Sovereign Gold Bonds - Invest in government-backed gold schemes.
  • National Pension System - Secure your future with NPS investments.
  • Public Provident Fund - Long-term savings with tax benefits.

Grievance Redressal & Support

  • Finance Helpline - Resolve issues related to tax, banking, and loans.
  • Fraud & Cybercrime - Report financial frauds and cybercrime.
  • RTI & Anti-Corruption - File RTI requests and report corruption cases.
